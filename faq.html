Frequently Asked Questions

What are the team rules?

Our team emphasizes respect, teamwork, and dedication. Archers are expected to attend practices regularly, follow coach instructions, and treat teammates and coaches with respect.

What is the dress code for practices and tournaments?

Archers should wear comfortable, non-restrictive clothing and closed-toe shoes. For tournaments, team uniforms are required and provided upon joining.

What are the team fees?

Team fees cover coaching, range access, and uniforms. Additional costs may include tournament entry fees and personal equipment.

What are the team expectations?

We expect archers to demonstrate commitment, attend practices, work on improving their skills, and maintain a positive attitude both on and off the range.
